Riverstone Schofields Junior Soccer Club, aka "The Gunners" is located at Schofields Park in Riverstone NSW and compete in the Blacktown and Districts Soccer Football Association (BDSFA) competition. We are a grassroots, not-for-profit community club run solely by volunteers who are passionate about football.

About Us

Opportunities for All Ages

With mixed team offerings for grassroots football (MiniRoos) from U5's through to All-Age's & competing in the prestigeous BDSFA Laing + Simmons Mens Premier League program - The Gunners has a football opportunity for you.
