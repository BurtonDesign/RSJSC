Life Members

Since its foundation in 1974 the Riverstone Schofields Junior Soccer Club has been run by volunteers who have unselfishly given up their time to ensure that people (young and old) in the Riverstone Schofields community have the opportunity to participate in the great game of football.

The following people have provided the club with outstanding service over a number of years and in recognition of their ongoing support, have been afforded RSJSC Life Membership.

Club Song

The Club's song was written by life member Mr Glen Cuthbert and emphasises the passion that he has for the club. Many a team have sung the club song after a hard-fought victory.

I am just a Schofields man
I will play as hard as I can
I love to wear the red & white
Because our team is dynamite
If you know the history
Your defeat is no mystery
Ask our supporters and they will say
Schoies know how to play!
