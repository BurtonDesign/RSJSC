Frequently Asked Questions

What team will my child be in?

Age groups 5-6 will be assigned to a team on muster day.
Age groups 7-16 will be graded into teams.

Muster days and Grading ‚Äì What's the difference?

Muster days for non-graded age groups are where your child, a coach and a manager are allocated to a team. Muster days are generally used for younger age groups who are just beginning their football journey.

Grading days are for graded age groups, where your child will take part in a few small games so our experienced club officials can ensure that all players are placed in an appropriate team that best fits their skills & abilities. This will ensure that your child will continue to develop their skills and can enjoy playing in a fair competition.

When does the season commence?

For junior teams, the standard winter soccer season begins at the end of March and finishes in mid-August. If your Childs team qualifies for finals, they will play for an additional 3 weeks.

Do games take place over school holidays?

Yes, the only weekends where no games are played are the Easter long weekend and June long weekend. June long weekend may be used for catch up games if games are rained out in weeks prior.

Where are games played?

RSJSC is a member of Blacktown District Football Soccer Association (BDSFA). Each team will have 'AWAY' games held at other fields within the district and 'HOME' games played at Schofields Parks.
