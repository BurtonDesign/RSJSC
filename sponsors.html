Thank you for your support

A message from the committee

The committee would like to thank the following businesses for their support of the Riverstone Schofields Junior Soccer Club. It is through the support of these local businesses that RSJSC has been able to keep club football low-cost and accessible for our community.

For this reason, we ask that all members and supporters show their appreciation for these businesses by utilising their products and services where ever possible.
